Before You Apply

Most Therapists Read About It.
I Lived It.

Most of the mental health industry is built on people who learned about anxiety, OCD, and depression in a classroom. They have the degree. They have the textbook. Most of them never actually transformed their own brain.

That's like a gym trainer who only read books about lifting and never put a single weight on the bar. Or a tennis coach who studied the game on paper but never picked up a racket. They can describe it. They can't feel it. So how would they teach you to do it?

I had OCD for 14 years. I lived in the loop. I built the framework I teach because I needed it to survive. Every tool I give you is something I tested on myself first. I have the muscle memory. I know what it feels like at 3am when your brain won't quiet down. I know what it takes to walk through that and come out the other side.

You don't need more letters after a name. You need someone who has been where you are and built a way out.
