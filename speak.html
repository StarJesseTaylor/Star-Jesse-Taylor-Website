Signature Talks

What Star Speaks About

Every talk can be delivered as a keynote, a half day workshop, or a full day intensive. Custom topics built for your audience on request.

Keynote · 45 to 75 min

Stop Reacting to Your Brain. Start Acting on Your Values.

The core emotional fitness talk. The framework that has helped thousands stop being run by anxiety, intrusive thoughts, and overthinking, and start choosing how to respond.

Keynote · 45 to 60 min

The Truth About Anxiety, Intrusive Thoughts, and Why Most Advice Fails

An honest look at what is actually going on inside the anxious mind, why most coping strategies make it worse, and what works instead. Built from 18 years of personal practice.

Workshop · 90 min to half day

The Value Garden: A Practice for Living on Purpose

An interactive workshop where every attendee builds their own Value Garden in the room. Teams leave with a tool they actually use, not a slide deck they forget.

Corporate · 60 to 90 min

Resilience That Actually Works at Work

For companies tired of meditation app subscriptions that no one uses. Real, practical emotional fitness for high pressure environments. Designed for leadership teams, sales floors, and burnout prone roles.

Retreat · Half day to full day

How to Be Your Own Best Coach

A deep, immersive day of practice. Attendees walk in carrying their own brain and walk out with the tools to coach themselves through whatever shows up next.
