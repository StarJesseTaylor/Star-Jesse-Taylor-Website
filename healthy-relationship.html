Why This Course Exists

Healthy relationships are hard.

Most people walk into relationships running the same patterns they have been running since childhood. They confuse anxiety with attraction. They make their partner responsible for their emotions. They communicate from a reactive place and then wonder why nothing changes.

This course is not about how to handle them. It is about how to handle you. Because the work always starts there.

When you change how you show up, the whole relationship has a different ceiling.
