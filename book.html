What's Inside

The Foundation of Everything Star Teaches

No fluff. No filler. The exact tools and shifts that take you from reacting to your brain to acting on your values.

The Value Garden

The core practice. You learn to identify what you actually value, plant those values, and water them with daily action. The single most important shift in the entire book.

The No Brain Method

Your brain is not the problem. Reacting to your brain is. You learn to notice your thoughts without obeying them, so you stop being run by anxiety, intrusive thoughts, and overthinking.

The Daily Practices

Simple, repeatable practices you can do every day. No 30 minute morning routines. Real tools that fit into a real life and actually move the needle.

How to Surrender

The misunderstood skill that changes everything. You learn the difference between giving up and letting go, and how to stop fighting your own life.

From Reacting to Acting

The full framework. You stop being a passenger to your emotions and become the person who chooses how to respond, even in the hardest moments.

How to Be Your Own Coach

Nobody is coming to save you. The book teaches you the exact way Star coaches himself every single day, so you can do it for yourself.

I wrote this book in the middle of my own transformation. Before any of the audience, before the coaching practice, when the only person I was accountable to was myself.

It is the distilled result of 18 years of practicing every major framework for human change, testing all of it, and discovering what actually works when you are alone in your own head at 3am.

Almost 2,000 people have started here. Many of them never needed coaching. The book did the work.

If you are tired of self help that sounds smart but does nothing, this is the one I would put in your hands.

— Star Jesse Taylor
