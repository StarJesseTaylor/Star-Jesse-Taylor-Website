There has never been more advice on how to live your life.
There has never been more people who feel confused.

That is not a coincidence.

Most self help is sold as a solution on top of a problem. It is
like a carpet that tries to cover up a hole. You are still going
to fall through the hole, because you have not built the
foundation underneath it.

Information is not transformation.

What changes you is the practice. He who seeks shall find.

This is why people who only collect information are wasting so
much time. They want to avoid the challenge of the practice. But
the people who actually practice go through difficulty in the
beginning, then they get stronger, and eventually everything gets
easier, because they built it on a strong foundation.

Here is one piece of the practice you can start today.

Your brain runs on algorithms. The patterns you spend the most
time in are the patterns that get the strongest. When you
ruminate, you feed those patterns. They start to run your life,
and you move through your day like a feather in the wind, blown
by whatever pattern woke up first.

The way out is the No Brain Method from my book.

When you wake up in the morning, you say it. No brain, we get up
right now. No brain, we walk in and brush our teeth right now.
No brain, we meditate right now. No brain, we talk to our friend
right now.

The moment you say no brain, you stop identifying with the
pattern. You step out of it.

Then you shift your focus to the present. We are walking right
now. We are doing the chest press right now. We are eating right
now.

Moment by moment, that becomes the new algorithm. A new
foundation. The old patterns go quiet because you stopped feeding
them.

This is what I teach.
