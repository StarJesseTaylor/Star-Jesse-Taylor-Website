# The Practice — Monthly Membership Offer

The continuity offer that closes the Hormozi gap in Star's business model. Built to launch June 1, 2026 (the day after the LA workshop).

---

## Naming

**Primary: "The Practice"**

Why:
- It is what Star already calls his work ("not theory, practice")
- 2 syllables, memorable, brandable
- Action-oriented (every page can say "Join The Practice")
- Scales as the offer grows (daily prompts, community, future tiers)
- Domain available: thepractice.starjessetaylor.com

Backup names if "The Practice" feels too generic:
- "Emotional Fitness Daily" (descriptive, less brand)
- "The Rewire" (outcome-focused, edgier)
- "Daily Reps" (fitness metaphor, action-focused)

## Price

**Monthly: $47/month**
**Annual: $470/year** (save $94, equivalent to 2 months free)

Why $47:
- $29 is too cheap for what Star can deliver. It sets a low frame.
- $97 is too expensive for cold conversion at scale.
- $47 is the sweet spot for premium creator memberships with serious offer stacks.
- It is also under the psychological $50 ceiling.
- Annual at $470 feels like a discount AND improves cash flow + reduces churn.

**Founding member rate for LA workshop attendees: $27/month for life**

Why this is justified:
- Anchors the in-room offer (something the LA crowd gets that nobody else can)
- Locks in the early adopters who will set the community culture
- Gives Star a "first 20 founding members" hook for the launch campaign
- $27 lifetime is profitable on $47 retail and the LTV is much higher than churn

## The Offer Stack (what members get)

1. **Daily 5-Minute Practice Prompt (Mon to Fri)**
   - Delivered every weekday morning to the Skool community
   - Each prompt: a single No Brain Method or Value Garden practice for the day
   - 5 minutes max, takeable on the commute

2. **Weekly Live Group Call with Star (60 min)**
   - Every Thursday, 6 PM Pacific
   - Q and A, hot seat coaching, live teaching
   - Recorded for members in different time zones

3. **Private Community (Skool)**
   - Members support each other between calls
   - Star is in the community daily
   - Members share wins, ask questions, get celebrated

4. **Monthly Themed Deep Dive Workshop**
   - First Saturday of every month
   - 90 minutes, one specific topic (anxiety, intrusive thoughts, relationships, work, etc.)
   - Replay added to the library

5. **The Practice Library**
   - All recorded past teachings, organized by topic
   - Members can search by what they are dealing with right now
   - Includes the No Brain Method audio library, the Value Garden walkthroughs, etc.

6. **Member Pricing on All Courses**
   - 50% off any course Star releases
   - Includes Self Worth, Anxiety & OCD, Foundation, future courses

7. **Live Event Member Pricing**
   - $50 off any future LA workshop
   - Priority booking before public

**Total retail value if priced separately: $400+ per month.**
**Member price: $47/month or $27/month for founding members.**

## Who It Is For (and Who It Is Not)

**For:**
- Book readers who want daily practice support
- People who finished a course and need accountability to keep practicing
- People who watch Star's videos but have not figured out how to actually use the framework
- People in the in-between phase: too far for the book alone, not ready for $1,997 1-on-1
- People who want community alongside their personal practice

**Not for:**
- People who want "fix me" energy. The Practice is for people doing the work.
- People who want 1-on-1 attention from Star directly. That is the coaching tier.
- People shopping for a course. This is a daily practice container, not a curriculum.
