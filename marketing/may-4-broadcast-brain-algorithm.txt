Quick teaching this morning before the emails go out.

Your brain has an algorithm. Just like TikTok.

Whatever you give attention to, your brain gives you more of. Even when you hate it.

You don't like the anxiety. You don't like the intrusive thoughts. You don't like the rumination. But you keep engaging with them. Trying to fix them. Analyzing them. Talking back to them.

Your brain does not know you hate them. It only sees that you keep showing up for them.

So it gives you more.

Most people try to fix this by fighting harder. That just feeds the algorithm.

The way out is how you change a TikTok feed. You change the channel.

You stop watching the penguin videos (anxiety, intrusive thoughts, rumination). You start watching the elephant videos (your valued actions). Walking. Working. Eating. Being present in your real life.

Moment by moment. That is what rewires the brain.

The tool from my book that actually does this is the No Brain Method.

"No brain, we are walking right now."
"No brain, we are eating right now."
"No brain, we are doing this work right now."

The moment you say "no brain," you separate yourself from the pattern. You stop being the pattern. You become the one watching it.
