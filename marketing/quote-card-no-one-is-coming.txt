================================================================
QUOTE CARD: "NO ONE IS COMING. I HAVE TO BE THERE FOR MYSELF."
For Instagram feed, IG Story, Threads, LinkedIn, Twitter/X
================================================================

This is your most quotable line. It is the emotional hinge of your
entire story. Use it everywhere.

----------------------------------------------------------------
WHY THIS LINE WORKS
----------------------------------------------------------------

- It is a decision, not a description. Decisions go viral. Descriptions don't.
- It names a universal moment most people have lived but never said out loud.
- It is YOUR line. Nobody else in the mental health space says it this way.
- It works as both a hook (cold audience) and a marker (warm audience nodding).
- It bridges your story to your offer. The decision IS the work.

----------------------------------------------------------------
DESIGN GUIDELINES
----------------------------------------------------------------

Build this in Canva (canva.com). 5 minutes.

CARD A: Instagram Square (1080 x 1080) — for feed posts
- Background: Black or deep cobalt (#1B6CA8) for emotional weight
- Quote text: White, bold sans-serif (Inter, Anton, Montserrat Black), centered
- Headline size: 70-90pt
- Attribution below: "— Star Jesse Taylor" in smaller white, 24pt, italic
- Optional bottom corner: small "@starjessetaylor" handle in white, 16pt
- LEAVE WHITESPACE. Do not fill the card. The line needs room to breathe.

CARD B: Instagram Story (1080 x 1920) — for stories + Reels covers
- Same composition, just vertical
- Add a soft cobalt glow behind the text or a subtle texture for depth

CARD C: Twitter / Threads (1200 x 675) — for landscape platforms
- Same line, landscape format
- Smaller scale, same minimalism
