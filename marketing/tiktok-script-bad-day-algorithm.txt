OPENING

Are you having a bad day mentally? That is why you are on here
scrolling. Well not anymore. In this video I am going to help you
not only have a better day today, but in general have better days.

THE REFRAME

First off, you do not have a bad day. Because if you have a bad
day, you have to wait till the next day to have a better day.

You have a bad MOMENT.

And do not worry, I know you have had a lot of bad moments
recently. But I am going to help you change the momentum.

YOUR BRAIN AS ALGORITHM

This is your brain. Your brain is giving you a lot of emotions
and thoughts recently. The problem is that your brain does not
understand that you do not like those emotions and thoughts. You
do not like them, but the brain does not know that, because you
are talking back to them, you are trying to fix them.

Your brain only knows when you engage with something.

So the more you engage with anxiety and intrusive thoughts, the
brain is not like, "oh you are hating these things." The brain
is like, "you are playing with the toys I am giving you." And
then your brain gets super excited. "Oh, you play with the toys
I created for you? Let me give you more excessive anxiety, more
depression, more thoughts you actually hate."

YOUR BRAIN IS LITERALLY THE TIKTOK ALGORITHM

Why did I just say algorithm?

Because your brain works exactly like the TikTok and Instagram
algorithm. Whatever you give watch time to, you get more of.

If you give watch time to penguin videos, the algorithm gives
you more penguin videos. The algorithm does not care if you do
not like them. You do not even comment. You just give them
constant watch time. And you are like, "why is this penguin
video on my screen, why are they riding crocodiles?" Meanwhile
you are giving it more watch time. The algorithm learns: they
love it. Even though you hate it.

It is the same with your brain. The intrusive thoughts. The
uncomfortable feelings. You give them so much watch time, even
when you are trying to fix them, even when you are trying to get
relief from them. Those behaviors make your mental health worse.

They have a specific name in the mental health industry.
Compulsions.

CUTTING OUT COMPULSIONS, THE WRONG WAY AND THE RIGHT WAY

We want to cut out compulsions because that is how we change the
algorithm and have a more beautiful screen time in life.

But how do we cut them out? Not by hyper focusing on them.
Because that becomes another addiction. "What compulsion do I
have? I have to cut them out!" And then your brain says, "oh you
love cutting out compulsions, here is more."

What we actually want to do is what the TikTok algorithm does.
Switch our focus to a new video. Change the channel.

THE SUPERPOWER

We use our superpower. Focus.

With focus we show our brain what we care about. And our brain
supports us in what we put our focus on.

Practically: I have two phones here. One represents the penguin,
the emotions and thoughts. The other represents the elephant,
the valued actions.

VALUED ACTIONS DEFINED

A valued action is an action that goes in your unique direction.
Your values, your excitements, your support.

Example: maybe you do not like your job right now. That is OK.
It still supports you. Keep doing it for the money. But over
time, shift in the direction of your excitement, what you
actually want to do. Maybe right now your excitement is playing
soccer, video games, making videos.

And your values? How you want to treat yourself. How you want to
treat others. Lovingly. Like Jesus said, the commandment is
love God and love your neighbors.

THE BIG SHIFT

This is the most important sentence in the video.

For better mental health, we have to switch our measure of
success. From "trying to get relief from emotions and thoughts"
to "living our lives with valued actions." Moment to moment.

That changes the algorithm in your brain.

The more you watch elephant videos moment to moment, the more
your brain supports you with ideas and inspiration for more
elephant videos. More support. More ideas for your business.
More ideas for your life.

THE LA WORKSHOP MENTION

I am getting out of breath because of how much value I am trying
to squeeze into this 6 minute video.

That is why you should come to my workshop in Los Angeles on May
30. That was a shameless plug. Because you cannot put all this
value and transformation in a 6 minute video.

PRACTICAL TOOLS FOR THE NEXT 24 HOURS

We talked about momentum. This word is so important. Right now
you have a certain algorithm momentum because you have spent so
much time on intrusive thoughts and uncomfortable emotions.

If you know how to change your social media algorithm, you know
it does not happen overnight. The algorithm says, "hey, you have
been watching penguin videos for years. Yeah, here are a few
elephant videos. But here are more penguin videos." That is part
of the process.

Your brain is not going to stop giving you emotions and thoughts
just because you start changing the momentum. Your brain will
test you. "You have been doing this for years. You must still
use this."

When those uncomfortable things come up, do not make them the
measure of success. Do not say "why are they still coming." Do
not check on them constantly.

NOT SUPPRESSION (CLOUDS ANALOGY)

Some people will say, "are we not suppressing the emotions and
thoughts if we just focus on the valued actions?"

No. We accept them. They are allowed to be there.

Example. I am driving to the gym. There are clouds in the sky.
I do not say, "oh I have to focus on the clouds now, I have to
practice accepting them by sitting with them." No. I just accept
them. I drive to the gym. The clouds are allowed to be there.
The trees are there too. I love trees.

Same way. When you go forward with valued actions, you do not
have to hyper focus on your emotions and thoughts. You just
accept them.

THE CORE PRACTICE

The penguin algorithm is created through REACTIVITY. Because you
react to emotions and thoughts.

The elephant algorithm is created through PROACTIVITY. Because
you proactively show your brain what you care about.

So tonight, set your valued actions proactively for tomorrow
morning. No matter what emotions and thoughts attack you
tomorrow, your measure of success is doing those valued actions
while you spend time outside of your head.

You can use the No Brain Method from my book.

"No brain, we are getting up right now."
"No brain, we are walking right now."
"No brain, we are on the chest press right now."

When you say "no brain," you differentiate yourself from the
pattern.
