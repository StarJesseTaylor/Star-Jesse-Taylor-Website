### Phase 2: Viral Push (May 11 to May 20)

Goal: Hit cold audience. Push for view counts, shares, saves.

Channels: TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts. Slideshow x2 per week on IG.

What to post:

- Slideshow Monday and Thursday on IG

- Email 2, 3, 4 in the sequence

CTA mix: 50% Free Chapter (capture cold leads), 30% Free Zoom or replay, 20% LA Workshop.

### Phase 3: Urgency and Close (May 21 to May 30)

Goal: Convert anyone on the fence.

CTA mix: 80% LA Workshop direct, 20% future content opt in for those who can't make it.

## Repurposing Strategy

Star already has 1B+ views worth of content. Do not start from scratch. The fastest way to fill 30 days:

1. Pull the top 30 performing reels from Star's existing TikTok and IG.
2. Re cut each with a new hook from the Hook Bank above.
3. Add a Phase appropriate CTA at the end.
4. Re post over the next 30 days.

This single move probably outperforms creating new content from zero.
