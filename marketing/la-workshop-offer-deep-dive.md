A full day immersive intensive. Not a lecture. A practice container. Here is the structure of the day:

The exact tool from the book, practiced in the room until your body knows it. By the time you leave, you can run this on yourself the next morning when the anxiety wakes up before you do. You will not have to remember the technique. It will be in you.

### Live Coaching And Hot Seat Work
I take real questions from real participants. Hot seat work for those who raise their hand. The teaching adapts to what is actually present in the room that day. This is the part you cannot get from any video, any book, any course. Your specific brain, in conversation with mine. The workshop is partly tailored to who is actually in the room.

### Surrender And Following Your Excitement
The Bashar and Michael Singer work distilled into something you can actually use. The difference between giving up and letting go. How to follow your excitement without losing your foundation. How to live in trust instead of in fear.

### How To Build An Atmosphere That Charges You Up

We work on the practical changes you can make to your environment and your daily flow so your atmosphere supports your healing instead of fighting it.

### Integration And Your 30 Day Plan
We integrate. You leave with a one page action plan written in your own hand. Not a slide deck. Not a PDF. Your actual plan for the next 30 days, built around your specific patterns and your specific life.
