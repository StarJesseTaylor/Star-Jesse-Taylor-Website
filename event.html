FAQ

The Questions People Ask.

What if I have never done in-person coaching before?
This is built for first-timers. You don't need to volunteer. You don't need to share. You can sit in the back, listen, take notes, and leave with the entire framework. The volunteers come from people who self-select. The teaching applies to everyone in the room equally.
Will the event be recorded? Can I get the replay if I can't attend?
Yes. The full event will be professionally filmed. Every ticket holder gets 14 days of replay access starting the Monday after the event. The teaching translates well to video so the replay is genuinely useful, but the room experience is irreplaceable.
What if I can't make it after I buy a ticket?
Tickets are non-refundable but fully transferable up until 48 hours before the event. If something comes up, gift your ticket to someone you love who needs to be in the room. We'll update the name on your behalf.
Is there a payment plan available?
For VIP tickets, yes. Reach out to starjessetaylor@gmail.com and we will set up a 2-payment split. General Admission at $97 is paid in full at checkout.
I have OCD / DPDR / panic attacks. Will the workshop trigger me?
The work is designed for people who experience exactly these patterns. Star teaches with care for the room. You can step out at any time. If you're worried about a specific situation, email us before booking and Star will reply personally.
Can I bring a friend or partner?
Yes. Each person needs their own ticket. If you're bringing someone, book your seats together so we can put you next to each other. Two GA seats next to each other is one of the most common bookings.
Will I be on camera?
The room will be filmed. By purchasing a ticket you authorize use of the footage for educational and promotional purposes. If you have concerns about being on camera, email us and we will seat you in a non-filmed section.
What is the difference between this event and your 1-on-1 coaching?
1-on-1 coaching is a 6 to 10 week journey through your specific patterns at $3,497 to $4,997. This event is a single afternoon of foundational teaching plus live demos at $97 to $347. Many attendees use this event as their first taste of working with Star and decide afterwards if they want to go deeper.
Will tickets be available at the door?
No. The room is capped at 30 seats. Once we sell out, the event is closed. Past events have sold out 7 to 14 days before the date.
