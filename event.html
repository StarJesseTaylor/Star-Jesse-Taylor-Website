What Happens In The Room

The Day Itself.
Six Phases.

Eighteen years of practice condensed into one day. Each phase builds on the last.

1

Getting Unstuck.

This is where we get you unstuck. We work on the limiting beliefs and patterns that have been running your life without your permission. After this you will see far more clearly what you want in life. You will be more authentic. More yourself. More expressive.

2

Direction. The One That's Actually Yours.

Now you have the clarity to set a direction for your life that's actually yours. In this section we do an exercise that gives you clarity on what is authentic you in life. Think of it as your GPS system.

3

Coaching.

We do live coaching in the room. I coach people on their patterns and we go deep.

4

Follow Your Excitement. Bashar's Formula.

Bashar's formula. Follow your excitement with no insistence on the outcome. I have been practicing this since 2011. In this phase we integrate it into your life.

5

Nervous System Regulation. Surrender.

I teach you how to lower the resistance in your body, balance your nervous system, and integrate the teaching of surrender. You leave with practical tools to regulate during the week.

6

New Beliefs. New Life.

Here we learn how to set new beliefs for your new life so you can move forward. I give you practical tools to use over the coming months.

Six phases. One day. The whole framework, lived.
