FAQ

The Questions People Ask.

Why is this only $97? Is there a catch?
Because I want the room full of people who actually need to be there. This is intentionally accessible. The teaching does not change because the price is lower. Same framework, same five hours, same room of 30. If you are reading this and wondering whether to come, the answer is yes.
I have tried therapy, books, and dozens of teachers. Why will this be different?
Because I lived 14 years of OCD and intrusive thoughts before I found something that actually worked. I read every book. I tried every method. None of it landed until I stopped collecting techniques and started practicing the one thing that ends the loop. This room is not theory. It is what I tested in my own life and what worked. You leave with the practice, not another book to add to the pile.
I have OCD, DPDR, or panic attacks. Will the workshop trigger me?
You are exactly who this room is built for. I lived 14 years of OCD, intrusive thoughts, and depersonalization before I built this work. The framework came out of needing something that actually worked when nothing else did. You are not the exception in this room. You are the audience.
What if I have never done in-person coaching before?
This is built for first-timers. You don't need to volunteer. You don't need to share. You can sit in the back, listen, take notes, and leave with the entire framework. The volunteers come from people who self-select. The teaching applies to everyone in the room equally.
Can I bring a friend or partner?
Yes, and it is encouraged. Doing this work alongside someone you love is one of the most powerful ways to integrate it. Book two tickets, sit together, leave changed together. Email us after booking and we will make sure your seats are placed next to each other.
What is the difference between this event and your 1-on-1 coaching?
1-on-1 coaching is a 6 to 10 week journey through your specific patterns at $3,497 to $4,997. This event is a single afternoon of foundational teaching plus live demos at $97 to $347. Many attendees use this event as their first taste of working with Star and decide afterwards if they want to go deeper.

Yes. The day will be professionally filmed for the replay every ticket holder receives, and select footage may be used in future marketing. By purchasing a ticket you authorize this use. If you would prefer to sit in a non-filmed section, email starjessetaylor@gmail.com after booking and we will reserve you a seat off-camera.
