FAQ

The Questions People Ask.

Why is this only $97? Is there a catch?
No catch. This is the first time I am running this format in LA, and I would rather have the room full of people who actually need to be there than priced for premium positioning. If you are reading this and wondering whether to come, the answer is yes.
Will you try to sell me something at the end?
Yes, and I will tell you exactly what before you buy a ticket. At the end of the event I will offer attendees the option to continue with me in a small private cohort. It is optional, it is for people who want to go deeper, and it has nothing to do with the value you get from the 5 hours in the room. The full event stands alone. You can leave changed and never spend another dollar with me. The cohort is there for the people who want more.
Is this religious or Christian?
No. This event is for everyone. I have studied every major tradition over 20 years, from Eastern philosophy to Western psychology to spiritual texts of every kind, and I draw from all of it. The work itself is universal. You do not need to share my beliefs, my background, or anyone else's in the room to benefit. The only requirement is that you actually want to do the work.
I have tried therapy, books, and dozens of teachers. Why will this be different?
Because the looking is the problem. Every method you have tried is teaching you a new technique. Another thing to do. Another reason your brain is the wrong size. This work does the opposite. It ends the search. The reason 20 years of seeking did not work is not because you have not found the right method yet. It is because the seeking itself is the loop. That is what the room on May 30 is built to break. You leave knowing what is real and what to actually do, not with another method to add to the pile.
What if I have never done in-person coaching before?
This is built for first-timers. You don't need to volunteer. You don't need to share. You can sit in the back, listen, take notes, and leave with the entire framework. The volunteers come from people who self-select. The teaching applies to everyone in the room equally.
Will the event be recorded? Can I get the replay if I can't attend?
Yes. The full event will be professionally filmed. Every ticket holder gets 14 days of replay access starting the Monday after the event. The teaching translates well to video so the replay is genuinely useful, but the room experience is irreplaceable.
What if I can't make it after I buy a ticket?
Refundable through May 23, one week before the event. Email starjessetaylor@gmail.com and we will process it. After May 23 the seat is committed but it is fully transferable, so you can send a friend in your place.
Is there a payment plan available?
For VIP tickets, yes. Reach out to starjessetaylor@gmail.com and we will set up a 2-payment split. General Admission at $97 is paid in full at checkout.
I have OCD / DPDR / panic attacks. Will the workshop trigger me?
The work is designed for people who experience exactly these patterns. Star teaches with care for the room. You can step out at any time. If you're worried about a specific situation, email us before booking and Star will reply personally.
Can I bring a friend or partner?
Yes. Each person needs their own ticket. If you're bringing someone, book your seats together so we can put you next to each other. Two GA seats next to each other is one of the most common bookings.
Will I be on camera?
The room will be filmed. By purchasing a ticket you authorize use of the footage for educational and promotional purposes. If you have concerns about being on camera, email us and we will seat you in a non-filmed section.
What is the difference between this event and your 1-on-1 coaching?
1-on-1 coaching is a 6 to 10 week journey through your specific patterns at $3,497 to $4,997. This event is a single afternoon of foundational teaching plus live demos at $97 to $347. Many attendees use this event as their first taste of working with Star and decide afterwards if they want to go deeper.
Will tickets be available at the door?
The room is capped at 30 seats. Once those are gone the event is closed. If you know you want to come, book in advance.
